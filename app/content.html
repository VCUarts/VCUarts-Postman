Please join me for coffee and pastries on October 1st from 8:30-9:30 a.m. on the 2nd floor of The Depot, 814 W. Broad Street, where we’ll catch up on all the happenings at VCUarts.

(If you haven’t had the chance to check it out, The Depot is a new space for students and faculty alike to meet, discuss and solve problems in groups, hang out, meet people and generally get things done.)

Coffee with the Dean is a once-a-semester event that gives me the opportunity to touch base with you, hear what’s on your minds, and also get your feedback on some new ideas that are floating around VCUarts. What makes this semester’s event different from previous years is our special guest, Allyson Vanstone, Dean of VCUQatar. Allyson is in town for VCUQatar Day, which will be September 30.

As I mentioned to you in my recent email, there’s a lot of talk about what we are teaching, what you are learning and how equipped you think you will be when you leave VCUarts. We’ll be talking about this and other things from 8:30 to 9:30.

No need to RSVP – just show up.

See you soon.
